About

About Me

I'm a UK-based video game composer with a passion for creating immersive, emotionally resonant soundscapes that enhance storytelling and gameplay. Drawing on years of personal gaming experience and formal study in composition, theory, and digital audio production, I specialise in crafting original music that supports the mood, pacing, and identity of a game world.
Whether you're looking for atmospheric background music, high-impact battle themes, or emotionally rich storytelling cues, I aim to deliver soundtracks that elevate the gaming experience and leave a lasting impression.
